Goodbye Ads and Trackers

Meet Netfilter

Netfilter is a highly efficient free and open source browser extension designed to block ads, trackers, coin miners, popups, anti-blockers, malware sites, and other unwanted content. It is widely regarded as the most advanced ad blocker in the world.

Ad Blocking

Netfilter is a must-have for anyone who wants to eliminate all the annoying banners, pop-ups that tend to clog up their browsing experience.

Privacy Protection

Netfilter ensures your online privacy by keeping your personal data safe from web analytics and trackers.

Browsing Security

Stay safe online with Netfilter - it protects against phishing scams and harmful websites. Browse stress-free knowing you're protected.
